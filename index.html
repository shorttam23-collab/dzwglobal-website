About

Dr. Tamara (Zai) Williams

The operator who built the system, then productized it.

Two decades in program management, operations, and acquisition strategy across U.S. government and private sector. High-stakes environments where the cost of poor execution was never hypothetical. The methodology I now bring to South African businesses was built across that career.

In 2025 I relocated from the United States to South Africa and took an investor position in a South African professional services business.
